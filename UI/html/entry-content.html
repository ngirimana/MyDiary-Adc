Title: My reflections on Andela EPIC’s value

Written on: 25/09/2019

Have you ever wondered why Andela is the best working community in Africa? Well, I am going to share Andela’s EPIC values which all andelans have. First of all, EPIC stands for Excellence Passion Integrity Collaboration. Let us go through each letter in this acronym to understand the values which the Andelan community is based on.

Excellence: When we say excellence for all andelans, it means that every andelan has an ambition which is to grow every day. Owning this value of excellence, all andelans are able to provide the best quality output. For andelans, being excellent is demonstrated in the following ways: before starting any sort of work, they first prepare their minds and prepare required tools so as to provide the best possible output. When an Andelan faces a blocker, he or she tries his best to solve the issue which sometimes requires seeking for help.

Andela community members believe that nothing is impossible which means that they are self-motivated. They also believe that where others say it is impossible, it is possible as long as you seek help and own your own learning. They believe that if they dream it, they have to make it a reality. Also, their excellence is shown by solving problems which benefit others. Andelans believe that if you believe in excellence as a value, actions talk louder than words. Work hard and learn so that you can be able to help others out. Doing more than what is expected, take initiative, use all possible resources available to solve any blocker and use the time available efficiently and you have a bright future ahead of you.

Passion: for all andelans, passion means doing what you love which means that they have to be passionate which goes hand in hand with being committed which is needed to achieve a lot in life. Being passionate means that you have to persistent where others have given up. In addition, every Andela community member believes that meeting a blocker increases his/her chances of success because it helps them to learn new things as a way of overcoming it. Having this value for all andelans helps them to set measures for keeping advancing where they fill like they are going to give up.

Integrity: In Andela, integrity means that they choose the right people to do the right things. Integrity in andelea’s values is anything that is more essential for them because it is bidirectional which means that every andelan should hold himself or herself and others accountable as others hold him accountable. All andeleans meant with a blocker should be open to others to ask them help and when they fail, they own it, learn from it and try their best to overcome failure. All andelans believe that integrity can be built and can be destroyed at any time and they should be kind, honest and authentic in society.

Collaboration: For andelans, collaboration means that their sum is greater than their parts. This value is very important in Andela because when one member gets stuck to anything, others are ready to offer help. In addition, these values lead to working in a team where every team member has a task to be accomplished, collaboration is applied here because if a team has a big task, they have to divide it into the small task and assign to every team member. Teams should be sure that every team member is working on his task and if he meets with a blocker, he can ask help from other teammates in order to complete the task with the right quality at the right time.
